Digi-Weirdo

led t-shirt that visualize your body movements

_

Digi-Weirdo is a wearable to explore establishing a statement of identity and guiding communication. By visualizing body movement throught LED matrix, people could communicate emotions as a digital person.

An individual project for Making Sense of Wearables.
