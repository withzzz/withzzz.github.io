To The Mountains

Experiments in Storytelling

_

(A work-in-progress project)

To The Mountains is an interactive transmedia documentary experience that puts the viewer in the driver's seat on a South American adventure following Ex-Pat Filmmaker, Sisa Bueno, as she films complex revolution while living in Bolivia.

On this website, the User will see a sleek and simple interface with an abstract map of Bolivia at the center of the screen with dots indicating specific locations. There is also video content of a jeep driving the dirt roads of Bolivia playing underneath the map image, putting the user in the driver's seat. On the map itself, the route is illuminated in sync with the location of the video so the User clearly understands their jeep's placement on the map. When the User arrives at a location dot, the webpage changes, and video clips pertinent to that specific location begin to play.

user flow

User will get a clue when watching the film. If users want to explore more video behind that scene, they can go to the website and click the dot matching to the clue.

compass design

The compass of the website is designed based on the route of Sisa's trip in Bolivia. I abstracted it into lines and dots to make it clearer to navigate, and also looks stylish.
