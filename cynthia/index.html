Concept

The story we tried to describe in this piece is about a girl’s dream between the fantasy and the reality world . During the interview we found the different interpretation of “Dream”, and it could be quite negative because the fact that the dream might be impossible to achieve and that’s why we want to make this girl somehow “invisible” in our project.

She compromises as she grows up and then she forgot her dream. So in the end the girl disappears and becomes particles.

A collaboration with Ava Huang.
