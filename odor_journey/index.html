Odor Journey

_

For my thesis project in ITP, I am working on bringing the sense of smell into design. To explore in this field I am asking questions like how we identify ourselves by smell and use different scent to mask our natural odor. How we communicate with smell, and the gap between what we expect and what others actually think about us. And the design opportunity in it.

This site shows the research, thoughts and creative works around my thesis project, around scent.
