PewPew!

A two-player strategy iPad game

_

PewPew! is a two-player strategy iPad game by drawing your bombs and walls within limited ink. The strategy is how you make good use of the ink to destroy your opponent’s base while protect yours. It’s simple, exciting and fun.

Team: Lirong Liu(developer), Zhen Liu(designer)
Software: Unity
Presented in ITP 2015 Spring Show, The NYC Arcade (2015)
