Zhen is an interaction designer pursuing her master degree in ITP, NYU. Her current research focus on bringing the sense of smell into design. She is collecting scent from people and exploring issues about how we communicate with ourselves and others through smell.

Prior to ITP, Zhen obtained her BA degree from Sun Yat-sen University where she formed a thinking of valuing the user experience. She interned as a UX designer in ARK design and SYSU Digital Media Research Center.

Her interest in multi-senses interaction was developed naturally after her exploration in VR experience design, big screens experience design and connected devices interaction design during her ITP life. Her projects have been shown at NYC Media Lab Summit, ITP Big Screens Show, Bodies in Motion show, Experiments in Storytelling, The NYC Arcade, ITP Spring/Winter Show.

She recently fell in love with floral design and perfume collecting. She also spends some time on her camera, sketching paper, guitar and green tea.
