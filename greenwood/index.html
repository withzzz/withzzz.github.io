Virtual Green-Wood

Data Art

_

A virtual interactive experience of the Green-Wood Cemetery. The data of 4944 soldiers are mapped in 3D space based on their location in the cemetery. Each rising particle represents the soul of a deceased soldier, creating a collective memorial. The experience takes place across devices from desktop, mobile to Google Cardboard.

Curator: Michele Gorman
